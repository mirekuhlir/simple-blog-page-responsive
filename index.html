Designer & developer

Hello,
my name is Mirek. I live in Brno, Czech Republic. I had my first computer, when I was twelve years old. I immediately started creating - web pages, graphics or modding games. I started programming at high school. My first programming language was Pascal, then I was programing in Delphi. I liked this programming language, really nice and clean syntax. I was fascinated by video games. I also wanted to create one. When the first iPad was introduced, it looked like a good platform for games. I discovered Unity and with friend of mine, who is a really good 3d graphic artist, we started making games. Over the years we have released several games of different genres.
The arrival of mobile platforms has brought so many new technologies. I love learning new things and I enjoy exploring new technologies. I was still looking for a new way of creating multiplatform apps. I found React and I immediately liked it. I gradually discovered other technologies from the JavaScript world.
